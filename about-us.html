About the Company

The Sun's Flower Shop has been proudly serving New Castle County since 1981 . We are family owned and operated with one location serving most of New Castle County, Elkton, Chadds Ford, Landenberg. We are committed to offering only the finest floral arrangements and gifts, backed by service that is friendly and prompt. Because all of our customers are important, our professional staff is dedicated to making your experience a pleasant one. That is why we always go the extra mile to make your floral gift perfect.
